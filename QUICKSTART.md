# Quick Start: "Hey Claude" Voice Automation

Get Claude opening with your voice in **2 minutes**.

## The Fastest Way (Recommended)

1. **Open Shortcuts app** on your iPhone
2. **Tap Automation** (bottom right)
3. **Tap +** to create new automation
4. **Select "Voice"**
5. **Say the phrase:** `Hey Claude`
6. **Tap Next**
7. **Tap "Add Action"**
8. **Search for:** `Open App`
9. **Select:** `Claude`
10. **Turn OFF** "Ask Before Running"
11. **Tap Done**

**That's it!** Now say "Hey Claude" and your iPhone will open Claude automatically.

---

## Alternative: Open Claude Website

If you don't have the Claude app, open it in Safari instead:

1. Follow steps 1-7 above
2. Instead of "Open App", use **"Open URLs"**
3. Enter: `https://claude.ai`
4. Tap Done

---

## Testing Your Automation

1. Say: **"Hey Claude"** (while holding your phone)
2. Your iPhone should unlock and open Claude
3. If it doesn't work:
   - Make sure "Ask Before Running" is OFF
   - Check that Siri is enabled in Settings
   - Ensure Claude app is installed

## Keyboard Shortcut Alternative

If voice doesn't work for you, add to Lock Screen:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Lock Screen**
2. Tap **Customize**
3. Add the Claude shortcut as a quick action button
4. You can now tap it directly from Lock Screen
