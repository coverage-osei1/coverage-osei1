About Ahafo Ano Community Bank

Ahafo Ano Community Bank PLC is a leading financial institution dedicated to providing comprehensive banking services to our community. We are committed to promoting financial inclusion, economic empowerment, and sustainable development through innovative financial solutions. With over 15 years of service, we have built a reputation for trust, reliability, and customer-centric banking.

Annual General Meeting Report 2024

AGM Meeting Summary

The 15th Annual General Meeting of Ahafo Ano Community Bank PLC was held on March 15, 2024, with shareholders, board members, and management in attendance.

Key Highlights

  • Approval of 2023 Financial Statements and Dividend Payment
  • Election of New Board Members for 2024-2026 Term
  • Presentation of 2024 Strategic Objectives and Business Plan
  • Discussion on Branch Expansion and Technology Infrastructure Upgrades
  • Community Impact Programs and Social Responsibility Initiatives

Resolutions Passed

  • Dividend of GHS 0.45 per share approved for 2023 financial year
  • Capital Expenditure budget of GHS 2.5 million approved for 2024
  • Reappointment of External Auditors (KPMG Ghana)
  • Approval of remuneration policy for executive management
  • Authorization to open additional branch in Kumasi

Chairman's Statement

"I am pleased to report that despite challenging economic conditions, our bank has demonstrated resilience and continued growth. Our asset base has grown by 18%, and we have successfully expanded our customer base by 12% in 2023. We remain committed to delivering exceptional service while maintaining strict regulatory standards and contributing positively to our communities."

— Kwame Asante, Chairman
